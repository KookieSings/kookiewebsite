Bio

BORK BORK! Arrrf! ARF! I'm a trans woman, stuck in a weird European Limbo. Singing about the joys and woes of my people. And, yes, sometimes about cherry shrimp.

I'm the co-founder of Transient Shift, a music event centered around trans music, run by trans people for trans people. More at transientshift.world.

Each of my albums is its own litle world. You could count them as concept albums probably.

  • Love, Time, Black Hole is a downtempo album about an astronaut and a mission control employee's doomed love. As they both throw themselves into a black hole in a bittersweet, final reunion.
  • Baby Trans is a musical journey through transition. The genre and production style of the music goes from classic rock to experiemental alt pop during the course of the album. The songs are compiled from both pre and post transition writings of mine.
  • Puppy Girl is a much bolder, higher energy album around the concepts of friendship, kinship, community and love. It.... HEY WAIT IT'S NOT OUT YET!

Peace, Love, Unity, Respect.
